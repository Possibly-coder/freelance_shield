## Tier 2: Where Big Clients Personally Read Posts

### 1. Hacker News (news.ycombinator.com)

YC founders, CTOs, VPs of engineering all read HN daily. One front-page post can get 5,000+ visits.

**Post a "Show HN":**

```
Title: Show HN: FreelanceShield -- Milestone-based escrow for freelance payments

We built a payment protection layer for freelancers and clients.
Client funds milestones into escrow (via Razorpay), freelancer delivers,
client approves, money releases. Auto-release timer if client ghosts.
Not a marketplace. 1-2% fee. Built with FastAPI + Next.js.

https://freelance-shield-olive.vercel.app
```

Post at 9 AM US Eastern (6:30 PM IST) for maximum visibility.

### 2. IndieHackers.com (the website, not Reddit)

The actual website has its own forum. Founders with $10K-$500K MRR businesses hang out there. Post a detailed product story with revenue goals and honest numbers.

### 3. Twitter/X -- Target Replies, Not Your Own Tweets

Don't just tweet into the void. Reply to people who are actively complaining.

**Search these queries daily on Twitter:**

```
"hired a freelancer" OR "freelancer ghosted" OR "freelancer scam"
"freelancer didn't deliver" OR "client didn't pay"
"upwork fees" OR "upwork takes 20%"
"how to pay freelancer safely"
"looking for freelancer" OR "need a developer"
```

When you find someone venting, reply with the short pitch:

```
Built something for exactly this -- milestone-based escrow for freelancers.
Money is locked before work starts, released only after approval.
If client ghosts, auto-releases in 7 days. freelance-shield-olive.vercel.app
```

**Indian startup founders to follow and engage with (they tweet about hiring constantly):**

- Founders with 10K-100K followers who build in public
- Search: "founder" AND "hiring freelancers" AND "India"
- A thoughtful reply from you gets seen by their entire audience

## Tier 3: LinkedIn DMs (Highest ROI Move)

Forget posting. DM directly. This is the single highest-conversion activity you can do.

### Who to target

Search LinkedIn for:

```
"Founder" AND "hiring freelancers"
"CEO" AND "digital agency"
"Head of Product" AND "contract developers"
"CTO" AND "outsource"
"Agency Owner" AND "India"
"Studio Founder" AND "design"
```

### The DM template

```
Hey [name], saw you run [company]. Quick question --
how do you currently handle payment protection when
you hire freelancers?

I built a tool for exactly this and would love 5
minutes of your feedback. Not selling anything,
genuinely want to know if this solves a real problem
for people like you.
```

This works because:

- You're asking for feedback, not selling
- Founders love giving feedback
- 80% will reply
- Then you demo FreelanceShield naturally

**Do 10 of these per day. That's 300 high-value contacts per month.**

### The follow-up (send 3 days later if no reply)

```
Hey [name], totally understand if you're busy.
Just wanted to share the link in case it's useful
for your team: freelance-shield-olive.vercel.app

Either way, would love to hear how you handle
freelance payments at [company]. Always learning.
```

## Tier 4: Partnership Strategies (Reach Thousands Instantly)

### Freelance Course Creators on YouTube

Dozens of YouTubers teach "How to Freelance" with 50K-500K subscribers. Every viewer is your target user.

**Approach:**

```
Hey [name], love your content on freelancing.

I built a free tool that protects freelance payments
using escrow -- thought it might be useful for your
audience.

Would you be open to mentioning it in a video, or
should I write a guest article for your blog? Happy
to give your students free early access as a perk.
```

**Find them:**

- YouTube search: "how to freelance in India", "freelancing tips"
- Look for channels with 10K-100K subscribers (they actually reply)
- Bigger channels (500K+) usually want payment -- skip them for now

### Startup Newsletter Writers

One mention in a newsletter with 1000+ subscribers = hundreds of signups overnight.

**Indian startup newsletters to pitch:**

- The Ken

- Blume Founders Newsletter
- Inc42
- Freshworks / Chargebee community newsletters

**Pitch:**

annually to payment defaults. No payment protection exists.

I'm building FreelanceShield, a Razorpay-powered escrow platform
for freelance payments. Currently in early access with [X] signups.

Would this make a good story for [newsletter name]?
Happy to share data, the founder story, and a demo.

## Tier 5: The Agency Play (One Sale = 50 Users)

Agency owners are the golden target. One agency has 10-50 freelancer relationships.

### Where to find them

- LinkedIn: Search "Digital Agency Founder India"
- Clutch.co: Browse top Indian agencies, find their founders
- GoodFirms: Same as Clutch
- Twitter: Search "agency founder" or "design studio"
- Instagram: Many design agencies have active Instagram presences

### What to offer them

```
Hey [name], I see [agency] works with a lot of
freelancers. We built a tool that handles milestone
payments with escrow -- so your freelancers get
guaranteed pay and you get guaranteed delivery.

Would save your team from chasing invoices.
Want me to set up a free account for your agency?
```

One converted agency = 10-50 freelancers onboarded automatically, because the agency will require all their freelancers to use FreelanceShield.

## The Real Secret

The biggest clients won't find you on Reddit or Twitter. They'll find you through **one of their freelancers** saying:

> "Hey, can we use FreelanceShield for this project? It protects both of us."

Every freelancer you sign up is a salesperson who brings their clients to your platform.

**Focus 70% of your energy on getting freelancers. The clients will follow.**
